Welcome

Taste Addis is a simple community guide to local restaurants, cafés, and cultural spots. Explore our reviews and recommendations to discover the best places in town.

Why Taste Addis?

Our goal is to help locals and tourists find authentic and trendy places around Addis Ababa. From traditional Ethiopian dishes to modern cafes and desserts, Taste Addis has got you covered.

Highlights

  • Authentic Ethiopian restaurants
  • Trendy cafs with great coffee
  • Delicious ice cream spots
  • Top cultural landmarks
  • Hidden gems you won't find in guides

About Us

Taste Addis was created by food lovers who want to share their favorite spots with everyone. Our team visits restaurants and cafes regularly to provide honest reviews and helpful recommendations.
